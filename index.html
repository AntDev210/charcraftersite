A SANDBOX/RPG style GAME,
ON THE TERMINAL like the OLD ONES!!!

CharCrafter is a sandbox/RPG game and programmed by a single developer,
AQUIL the eagle.
The game takes inspiration from old command-line games, where the player interacts with the story through various inputs. And, like sandbox games, the player must not simply follow a story path, but must follow their own decisions and intentions, so as not to repeat the same path as other players, but to decide their own.
